## Restructured Methodology: Critical Realism's Stratified Ontology

**Overall System: Critical Realism's Stratified Ontology (비판적 실재론의 층화된 존재론)**
This conceptual model describes three nested, concentric ontological strata of reality. Its purpose is to illustrate the generative depth of reality, emphasizing that empirically observed events constitute only a subset of actual events, which are themselves generated by underlying structures and generative mechanisms.

---

**Components:**

*   **Ontological Stratum: The Real (실재적 층위)**
    *   **Description:** The outermost and largest region of reality. This stratum encompasses entities, structures, and generative mechanisms that exist independently of whether they are observed or not.
    *   **Content Label:** "구조 · 생성기제" (structures and generative mechanisms)
    *   **Visual Characteristic:** Represented as the largest concentric region.

*   **Ontological Stratum: The Actual (현실적 층위)**
    *   **Description:** The middle region of reality, fully contained within The Real. This stratum comprises the events that occur when generative mechanisms operate under specific conditions.
    *   **Content Label:** "발생한 사건" (events that occur)
    *   **Visual Characteristic:** Represented as a medium concentric region, fully enclosed by The Real.

*   **Ontological Stratum: The Empirical (경험적 층위)**
    *   **Description:** The innermost and smallest region of reality, fully contained within The Actual. This stratum represents the subset of events that are actually observed by humans.
    *   **Content Label:** "관측된 사건" (observed events / observations)
    *   **Visual Characteristic:** Represented as the smallest concentric region, fully enclosed by The Actual.

---

**Groupings:**

*   **Stratified Ontology Model:**
    *   **Members:** The Real (실재적 층위), The Actual (현실적 층위), and The Empirical (경험적 층위).
    *   **Arrangement:** These three strata form a single, cohesive model depicted as three nested concentric regions (circles or rounded rectangles).
    *   **Containment Hierarchy:** The Real forms the outermost layer, The Actual is fully contained within The Real, and The Empirical is fully contained within The Actual, forming the innermost layer.
    *   **Visual Emphasis:** To convey the "generative depth" message that "only a small subset is observed," the outer rings (The Real and The Actual) must be visibly much larger than the inner ring (The Empirical).

---

**Data Flow (Generative Direction):**

*   **Flow Path:** A single causal/generative directional arrow explicitly crosses the three strata from OUTSIDE to INSIDE.
*   **Source:** Structures and Generative Mechanisms (originating within The Real).
*   **Intermediate Step:** Generation of Events that Occur (manifesting within The Actual).
*   **Destination:** Observation of Events (a subset of which are accessible within The Empirical).
*   **Directional Arrow Labeling:** The flow "mechanism → event → observation" must be clearly readable, pointing inward.
*   **Meaning:** Generative mechanisms produce events, and a subset of those events is subsequently observed.

---

**Input/Output (Conceptual):**

*   **System Input (Conceptual):** The inherent existence and operation of "구조 · 생성기제" (structures and generative mechanisms) within "실재적 층위" (The Real).
*   **System Output (Conceptual):** The "관측된 사건" (observed events / observations) that are accessible within "경험적 층위" (The Empirical).

---

**Key Relationships:**

*   **Containment Relationship:**
    *   **Description:** A fundamental set/subset containment model, where each inner stratum is entirely encompassed by the one immediately outside it.
    *   **Explicit Relation:** The Real contains The Actual, which in turn contains The Empirical.
*   **Generative Depth (발생적 깊이):**
    *   **Description:** The core message of the model, highlighting that what is empirically observed is fundamentally only a SUBSET of the full reality (structures, mechanisms, and all actual events).
    *   **Visual Reinforcement:** The significant difference in size between the outer and inner concentric regions is crucial for conveying this principle instantly.
*   **Causal/Generative Link:** The direct causal connection where the operation of generative mechanisms leads to the occurrence of events, a portion of which then become observable.

---

**Sequential vs Parallel Operations:**

*   **Sequential Generation:** The process of generation, from mechanisms to events to observations, is conceptualized as a sequential, directional flow.
*   **Parallel Existence:** The three ontological strata (The Real, The Actual, The Empirical) are understood to exist simultaneously as distinct, yet nested, layers of reality.

---

**Mathematical Operations:**

*   **Set Inclusion:** 실재적 ⊇ 현실적 ⊇ 경험적 (The Real is a superset of The Actual, which is a superset of The Empirical).
